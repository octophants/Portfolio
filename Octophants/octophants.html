My obsession with tentacles began three years ago, after a sleep-deprived night of working at the AMT Lab in Parsons. I was drowning in an ocean of work which wasn't particularly interesting, when I suddenly realized that I really wanted to draw some tentacles. Soon, I began seeing tentacles everywhere. I walked into my apartment and all I could see were tentacles â€“ all over the walls, twisting down the stairs into the basement, slithering out from under the couch. The name 'Octophants' arose from a combination of tentacles and elephant trunks, which is a recurring theme in my work. I now spend my days in quiet contemplation of tentacles and their existence in my life. I don't think they will ever leave me, but at least I can keep making cool art.
